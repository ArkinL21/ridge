About Us

Ridge Dental Practice was born out of a deep passion to serve and make a difference in our community of Katima Mulilo. For many years, our town faced challenges in oral health care, especially in the private sector, with limited access to quality dental services.

We saw the need for a caring, trustworthy dental provider that truly understands the needs of our people. With this purpose in mind, Ridge Dental Practice was founded to bring modern, compassionate dental care.

We wanted to create a warm, welcoming space where everyone feels comfortable and cared for. Our team is dedicated to restoring smiles, improving health, and building lasting relationships with our patients.

Our journey is about more than just dentistry — it’s about giving our community a reason to smile confidently and healthily. We are proud to be part of Katima Mulilo’s growth and well-being, and we look forward to serving Katima Mulilo and its surrounding areas for many years to come.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to prioritizing your health through high-quality care, using advanced technology and a patient-centered approach. Our aim is to improve your quality of life through education, prevention, and personalized treatments.
