We are excited to welcome you to Ridge Dental Practice

We are a family practice located at the heart of Katima Mulilo. At Ridge Dental Practice, our experienced team prioritizes our patients’ wellbeing through the delivery of high-standard health care.

We are dedicated to using advanced technology, evidence-based dentistry, and a patient-centered approach. We cater to all our patients’ needs, from routine check-ups to advanced dentistry such as cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and prosthetics.
