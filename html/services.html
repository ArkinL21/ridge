Our Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care for individuals and families. We provide modern treatments with a gentle, patient-focused approach.

Comprehensive Care

At Ridge Dental Practices we provide a wide range of dental services designed to keep your smile healthy and confident. Our experienced team uses modern technology and gentle techniques to ensure every patient receives comfortable, high-quality care.

medical_services

General Dentistry

  • Comprehensive Dental Examinations: Detailed assessments to detect potential issues early and keep your smile healthy.
  • Regular Checkups: Routine visits to monitor and maintain your oral health.
  • Professional Cleaning: Deep cleaning to remove plaque and tartar, helping prevent cavities and gum disease.
  • Fillings: Restoring teeth damaged by cavities using durable materials.
  • Root Canal Treatment: Saving infected or damaged teeth by removing pulp and sealing the inside of the tooth.
  • Preventive Care: Guidance and treatments to help prevent future dental problems.
sentiment_satisfied

Cosmetic Dentistry

  • Teeth Whitening: Brightening your smile by removing stains and discoloration.
  • Veneers: Thin porcelain shells placed over teeth to improve shape, colour, and appearance.
  • Crowns and Bridges: Crowns cover damaged teeth, while bridges replace missing teeth by anchoring to neighbouring teeth.
  • Dental Implants: Titanium posts placed in the jawbone to support natural-looking replacement teeth.
straighten

Orthodontics

  • Braces: Metal or ceramic devices attached to teeth to gradually straighten and align them.
  • Clear Aligners: Removable, nearly invisible trays that gently shift teeth into the correct position.
dentistry

Prosthodontics

  • Dentures: Custom-made removable or fixed appliances that replace missing teeth and restore chewing and speaking confidence.
healing

Oral Surgery

  • Extractions: Removing damaged, decayed, or impacted teeth that cannot be saved.
  • Minor Surgery: Simple procedures such as biopsies or management of infections like abscesses.
emergency

Emergency Care

  • Urgent Dental Care: Immediate response to fractures, cuts, broken teeth, swelling, and bleeding to relieve pain and prevent further damage.
